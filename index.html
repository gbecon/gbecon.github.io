About

I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at Ahmedabad University. I have a PhD in Economics from Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi, India. My doctoral dissertation explores the political economy of trade and environmental policies, highlighting the role of lobbying in shaping policy decisions.

My research interests broadly reside in environmental economics, political economy, and development, focusing on how political incentives, governance, and institutional quality shape development outcomes.
